The Archive of Allergies

Mangos

Mangos used to be my favorite food but I'm actually really allergic to mangos. I used to just get horrible hives and rashes, which I would just tough it out because I loved the taste of mango so much, but ever since I developed asthma my freshman year at UCSD, I now get asthmatic reactions. One time I accidentally ate a small piece of mango on a fish taco with mango salsa at Segundo Dining Halls at UC Davis and I started to wheeze a bit, so the dining hall manager made me sign a form consenting it was my mistake for not reading the labels clearly.

Avocados

I'm somewhat moderately allergic to avocados as well. This relates back to my mango allergy as both are from similar fruit families. I get sligtly asthmatic with this allergy as well, but with this specific allergy, my eyes tear up like crazy. Everytime I go out to eat poke bowls and sushi, which are two of my favorite foods, I always have to ask if there is avocados in the food and if they can make it without avocado. It's pretty sad. :/ It's okay, though. I dont particularly like avocados anyways.

Dogs

I'm actually really allergic to dogs. I can tolerate dogs for about 0.5-1.0 hours before I start feeling like im dying. I can only admire them from afar after that. ): IT'S SO SAD BECAUSE I LOVE DOGS SO MUCH. I really want a dog sometime after I graduate, so I've been considering allergy shots because I want a fluffy companion, specifically a corgi, who will give me unconditional love and never disappoint me. :)

Cats

I'm actually really really allergic to cats. I get an immediate allergic reaction though short contact or even walking into an environment that a cat lives in. It's not only an asthmatic reaction, but the whole shebang of wheezing, watery eyes, sneezing, runny nose, and itchyness. It's pretty intolerable. I literally can only admire them from afar. IT'S SO SAD BECAUSE I ALSO LOVE CATS A LOT. I considered getting a cat but I don't think I could handle the allergic reactions even if I started allergy shots. My dream when I was younger was to get an orange tabby cat, but that dream has now died. :)

Nickel

I'm allergic to nickel and similar metals to it as well. I can't wear "cheap" jewelry for more than a few hours before i start getting really itchy. I can only wear probably certain purities of silver, gold, or platnium. It's really sad because I want to accessorize like everyone else does. ;-; RIP me. But I guess it saves me some money... but not really since I still want to accessorize and all my jewelry have to be gold and costs me $100+ for every piece...
